Meri's Approach

Meri has developed a method of introducing clients to the shamanic journeying process through a practical and deeply meaningful step by step approach. Such approach is accessible privately and in a group format. Once the skillset of the shamanic journey is mastered, clients who are interested will receive gentle guidance and powerful support as they delve into core shamanic healing.

While some Shamanic work utilizes plant medicine, a requirement of my practice and core shamanism is that we explore without any mind altering substances.
